<h2><u>Viewing Subtitles</u></h2>
Some of&nbsp;the videos on Hummedia have optional subtitles. In order to toggle these subtitles on/off, please use the CC button on the bottom right corner of the video player.&nbsp;
<br><br>
<img src="../img/help/viewing-subtitles/1.png" width="90%">
<br><br>
On iOS devices, the button to toggle subtitles is found in the bottom right corner after making the video fullscreen.
